B2B=B2C?

A trend that has been emerging for a long time is the merging of B2C and B2B. Because even in the business customer context, research and information is usually first researched online before the company is contacted. The typical cold calling is no longer necessary. The quality of the research is becoming more and more decisive for a purchase. Intuitive usability and an appealing design, as customers from the B2C environment are used to, are not only expected, they are actually required. The same type of online experience must be created in B2B in the future. In order to recreate this B2C experience, many companies have to retrofit - in terms of design and usability as well as the tools and information that they give their customers.
